About the Livingstone Company

There's only one right way to do things.

The Livingstone Company was founded in 1976 by the president, Jack Livingstone as a painting company.

Over the years the company has evolved into building, remodeling, and additions.

Since 1989 the company has expanded into land development and new home building. Completed projects include Hyland Crossing, Pheasant Hill Reserve, and Whitcomb Pines condominiums all in Scituate.
